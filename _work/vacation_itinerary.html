THE AMAZING TOKYO

Tokyo - Ginza - Asakusa - Funabashi

Itinerary for 5 days 4 nights

Day 1

Vancouver - Tokyo - Ginza

On landing at Narita International Airport, after reclaiming your luggages and clearing customs, you are anticipated to join our welcome event directed by Halona guide. After that, we transfer you to a luxurious hotel to check-in. The rest of your day will be free for you to rest or go explore the hotel and go around Ginza which is within walking distance from the hotel. A full day of scheduled sightseeing awaits you tomorrow.

Day 2

Tokyo - Asakusa - Sensoji Temple - Firework Display

The first stop today is the impressive Asakusa and the temple in the area, Sensoji Temple. The temple which could hold 1,000,000 people at a time, is usually visited during New Year where traditional Japanese prays for good luck and make a wish. Sensoji Temple is the largest ancient palace in Tokyo area. We will spend the rest of the day here and close the night with spectacular firework display. Our suggestion is to explore as much as you can around Asakusa way to the temple as there is a lot of local small shops that sell various interesting japanese snacks and gifts.

Day 3

Universal Studio - Yakisoba Dinner

Today's exploration begins with a visit to the Universal Studio Japan, a fantastic theme park that offers a lot of attraction and rides. Lunch will be provided on site, where all our tour participants need to gather at Jurassic Park Cafetaria which is located in the middle of the theme park. After that, you are free to spend the rest of your day in the park. We will gather back at the gate at 7 PM and we will proceed to our favorite recommendation, Yakisoba Dinner at Tokyo Central Park. We will close our long day after dinner, and go back to the hotel to rest.

Day 4

Disneysea - Spanish Village - Snow Monkey viewing at natural onsen

This morning you will need to wake up early and eat breakfast at the hotel breakfast buffet. We will then proceed to go to Disneysea. We will spend half of the day in DisneySea today and then go to our next destination, Spanish Village. Our hotel for the day will be located at Spanish Village. You need to make sure your family gathered again in front of the hotel gate at 4 PM where the bus will pick us up to go to monkey viewing at natural onsen an hour from Spanish Village. After this we will go back to the hotel to enjoy our Spanish Dinner and rest.

Day 5

Okonomiyaki Lunch - Ryokan Ugenta

Today we will head back to our lovely Vancouver. In the afternoon, we need to gather in the lobby and the bus will pick us up to enjoy the renowned Okonomiyaki Lunch. We will also have a brief time to visit Ryokan Ugenta on the way to the airport. Ryokan Ugenta is a very famous japanese traditional inn that is already been around for five generations. We are very happy that you chose Halona and hope we will see you again in our next adventure.
